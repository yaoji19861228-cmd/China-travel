Good to know

Straight answers before you book.

Clear expectations make a better first day. If your situation is unusual, send it with your travel dates.

Is airport transfer included in the $59 session?

No. The $59 core product is the face-to-face travel guidance session. NIO ES8 airport pickup or drop-off is an optional $39 add-on, with route, time, luggage and meeting point confirmed in advance.

Can you guarantee payment-app approval?

No. Approval depends on the platform, your identity details and your card issuer. I guide the setup, help you understand the flow and prepare sensible fallback options.

Is this a tour guide service?

The core session is independent travel strategy and practical setup, not licensed attraction guiding. When ticketed scenic guiding requires a licensed guide or qualified agency, that must be arranged separately.

What should I send before booking?

Your travel dates, cities, arrival airport and time if relevant, hotel area, number of travelers, travel style, must-see places, food restrictions and the parts of China travel that currently feel confusing.

Are extra costs confirmed before service?

Yes. Airport route, waiting time, late-night timing, parking, tolls, overtime, suburban travel and special requests are discussed before you confirm. Meals, tickets and personal purchases remain separate unless stated.
